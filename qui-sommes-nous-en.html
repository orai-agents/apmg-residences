About Us

APMG Residences is a family-owned real estate firm specialized in the acquisition, transformation and valorization of high-end properties located in Cannes and on the French Riviera.

Founded by Arnaud and Pauline, the company is part of a demanding approach: creating premium residences by completely rethinking apartments and houses selected for their location, potential and character.

We work in close collaboration with architects and interior designers to ensure a clear and coherent vision for each project.

Each renovation is designed as a true architectural intervention, where light, volumes, materials and circulation are completely rethought to offer a modern, elegant and perfectly optimized living experience.

We adopt a structured real estate vision, anchored in trust, rigor and impeccable discretion.

Owners, buyers, investors, we support you with a rare degree of excellence, where each project is a unique creation.

Our Activity

APMG Residences operates throughout the entire cycle of high-end real estate transformation.

Our business consists of identifying exceptional properties, rethinking their architecture alongside professionals, carrying out meticulous renovations and ensuring their final valorization on the market.

Our approach follows a clear and structured process, designed to guarantee a coherent, elegant and perfectly controlled result.

Targeted Acquisition

We select properties located in the most sought-after locations in Cannes, with strong architectural potential or notable heritage value. Each acquisition is based on rigorous analysis: volumes, light, circulation, transformation potential.

Architectural Study

We collaborate with architects and interior designers to completely rethink the spaces. Plans, surface optimization, choice of materials, stylistic coherence: everything is designed to create a remarkable residence.

High-End Renovation

Our projects are characterized by noble materials, impeccable finishes and contemporary design. We supervise each stage to guarantee a result faithful to our aesthetic and qualitative requirements.

Sale or Temporary Rental

Properties are offered to our network or to selected partner agencies for their professionalism. When relevant, we temporarily rent the property to enhance it or optimize its management during the sales period.

The Essential

Our activity is based on a precise vision: identify properties with strong potential, transform them with high architectural standards and valorize them through high-end achievements.

For each project, we implement a structured approach, where the selection of locations, the design of spaces and the quality of finishes are thought of as a coherent whole.

This approach allows us to create unique, elegant and sustainable properties, capable of seducing a demanding clientele concerned with quality.

We operate with rigor, discretion and personal involvement, to guarantee an impeccable result and a true APMG signature.

Transform, sublimate, valorize: this is how we bring exceptional properties to life.
